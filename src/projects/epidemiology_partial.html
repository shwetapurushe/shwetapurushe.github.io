An Open source visual analytic platform for epidemiological data analysis.

The development of the Weave Analyst was guided through the ‘Agile’ process of development by the Department of Public Health, Seattle King County (SKC), Centers of Disease Control and Prevention (Atlanta) and the Rhode Island Data Hub.

Design and Implementation overlap with the Weave Analyst with the following additional goals
  • Categorical and time data filters
  • D3 geographical filters
  • Additional support for epidemiological routines, for example prevention of record identification, remapping data etc.
